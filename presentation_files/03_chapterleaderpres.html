Responsibilities

  • Chapter summary
  • Extra material
  • Exercises

Chapter summary

  • Before: Read chapter and summarise in point form
  • During: Talk through interesting points
  • After: Add new points/unresolved questions to wiki

Extra material

  • Before: Research related material and add links to wiki
  • During: Use asides as talking points
  • After: Put any extra links on wiki

Exercises with answers

  • Before: Invent/find suitable questions and write sample answers/tests
  • During: Facilitate discussion and whiteboard/projector presentation of group answers
  • After: Post answers on wiki

Benefits

  • Different perspectives
  • Consolidate learning
  • Practise new vocabulary

What to do differently

  • Set fewer exercises
  • Prepare answers before releasing exercises
  • Ensure exercises ready at prior session
  • Do more external research
  • Ensure post-session work done
